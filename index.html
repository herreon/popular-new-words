In 2019, these were the top new darlings in colloquialism: SFS, VSCO, SB, NGL, S/U, FYP, IYKYK, simp, and ok boomer.

From the end of 2018 to June 2019, SFS led the pack by a significant margin, followed by VSCO, SB and NGL.

Soon after June, VSCO surged in search interest, reaching peak popularity in September.

In the final quarter of 2019, ok boomer outstripped all other new words. Interest dwindled somewhat after January 2020, but remained the most popular search by far.

2020 so far has seen the unprecedented rise of simp, exceeding even ok boomer in popularity.
